VX3000 Radio 001: Sekia Sessions

A high-brow mix of the best that downtempo, deep house, and liquid jungle has to offer, Sekia Sessions is a trip through the sophisticated side of electronic music. Meant to relax and inspire, the tracks are sourced from all over the globe, with an emphasis on Japan and America. This is my first mix under the official 'VX3000 Radio' umbrella, but it's already gained some significant attention. If you're watching this, thanks for supporting me.



VX3000 Radio 000: My God, These Kids Are Bigger Shitheads Than I Thought

Akin to a walk through a snowy suburban neighbourhood, Shitheads is hazy and slightly depressing. It's a collection of fringe 90s rock; some emo, some punk, as long as it has guitars and drums and someone screaming their heart out.
